Kingston Locks

197 Black Hall Road Kissy Freetown

Mhizz Bie's Fashion Boutique offers elegant, trendy styles that celebrate every woman’s unique beauty. It’s where fashion meets confidence and class.

Kingston Market

The Kingston Public Market, 216 Ontario St

The Kingston market opens in the downtown market square most saturdays. Vendors from local businesses populate the stalls, and provide the opportunity to shop local producs.

Kingston Penitentiary

Kingston Penitentiary, 560 King St W

The Kingston Penitentiary is a former maximum security prison that now offers tours. The Penitentiary has been used as a set to film several movies and shows including Mayor of Kingstown, DC's Titans, and Star Trek: Discovery.

Kingston Downtown

Kingston sign, 1095 Ontario St

The Kingston sign is a tourist attraction great for pictures and starting your journey through downtown Kingston.

Lemoine Point

Lemoine Pt Trl, Kingston, ON

The Lemoine Point Trail is a great spot of nature for those who enjoy walking trails. Bring some seeds to feed the birds!

Confederation Park

Confederation Park, Kingston

Located near the Kingston Sign, Confederation park features a wide view of the waterfront and docks. It is also located close to the Wolfe Island Ferry.

Fort Henry

Fort Henry National Historic Site, 1 Fort Henry Dr

Fort Henry provides a window into the past, featuring actors and artifacts from when the fort was in use. Cannons, soldiers, and beaver tails make the fort a must. The fort also hosts events during halloween like Pumpkinferno, and Fort Fright.

Beaver Tails

BeaverTails- Queues de Castor (Kingston Market St.), 20 Market St

Beaver Tails is a great place for those with a sweet tooth, featuring many variations of the popular friend pastry. Another place to string along your downtown adventure, come prepared for sticky, sugary goodness.
